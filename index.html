About Calm Smiles Oklahoma

Calm Smiles Oklahoma is a public resource created to make it easier for anxious Oklahomans to find dental care that feels safe, respectful, and affordable.

  • Highlights clinics that openly welcome anxious or long-avoiding patients
  • Shows insurance options, including SoonerCare and major PPO plans
  • Shares simple tools to feel more in control before and during your visit

What is Dental DJ and how does it help with anxiety?

Dental DJ is a simple web tool some offices use to let patients queue songs from Spotify in the waiting room or operatory. When you can hear music you chose, it can:

  • Give you something to focus on besides the procedure
  • Make the room feel more familiar and less clinical
  • Help you communicate your mood without having to talk a lot
